I'm the guy who doesn't stop until it's fixed. I'm a problem solver. This is how I ended up here, pursuing something I love. I spent a lot of time feeling stuck in positions that provided for my family, but left me bored, and discouraged. It took a lot of sacrifice to make a career change, but I'm excited about what I've learned, and the good that will come out of doing something I enjoy.

I like to make things. When I was a kid I loved to play with Legos, draw, and play the air guitar while shouting "Born in the USA!" I love to write songs, and make music, and I love designing logos, web sites, and sermon graphics for my church. Nothing inspires me like creativity, and it is my joy to create.

Henry Ward Beecher said, "In this world, it is not what we take up, but what we give up, that makes us rich." People that believe this, and live this way, make the world a better place. I wouldn't have this opportunity to make a career change if my wife wasn't willing to make the necessary sacrifices for me to do so.
I wouldn't have learned so much in such a short period of time if my mentor, Chris Courtney, wasn't willing to give his time and expertise to my development as a Designer.
I've been given so much, and I can't wait to give everything I've got to my future team, and future projects.

""Success is not final; failure is not fatal: It is the courage to continue that counts."
