ABOUT ME

I was born with a passion for creativity. My whole life it has been a part of me, and has been expressed through music, drawing, and more recently in designing digital spaces. Georg Hegel said, "Nothing great in this world has ever been accomplished without passion." Let's just say I've got a very solid foundation I'm building on.

Colossians 3:23 says “Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord…” Whether you're looking for someone to help develop your brand, or improve your web site or app, you can count on me to invest my passion and heart into your projects.

Henry Ward Beecher said, "In this world, it is not what we take up, but what we give up, that makes us rich.” It is my goal to live this way everyday, looking more for what I can give, than what I can take, because as Jon Foreman (one of my favorite songwriters) says, “This skin and bones is a rental.”
