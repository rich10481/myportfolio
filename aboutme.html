I chose design because it’s fun, and I spent enough time bored already! So we (my amazing wife and I) decided to take all the money we had and invest it into a new career that would be fun so that I could be healthier, and happier, but we didn’t know exactly which direction to go. Art has always been a big part of my life, whether in the form of drawing, playing music, or writing songs, I’ve always had a creative outlet no matter what job I was working to pay the bills. So I chose Design with my wife’s support and I’ve been having fun with it ever since!

Colossians 3:23 says “Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord…” Whatever I find myself doing, I’m completely invested in it. If I wasn’t that way I could never have accomplished what I have in the past 6 months. Because I live by this principle, I know I’ll be a great asset to whatever team I end up on.

Henry Ward Beecher said, "In this world, it is not what we take up, but what we give up, that makes us rich.” It is my goal to live this way everyday, looking more for what I can give, than what I can take, because as Jon Foreman (one of my favorite songwriters) says, “This skin and bones is a rental.”
