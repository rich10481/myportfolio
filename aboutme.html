MY STORY

When you find yourself in your early 30s and happy with everything but your job, it isn’t easy to take a leap of faith and change careers. Especially when that leap of faith requires investing what would have been your down payment on a home, into learning new skills to pursue your passion. When your beautiful, wise, and very practical wife supports making such an impractical leap, you know it’s the right time.


With a lifelong passion for creativity and problem solving, I set out to find the field that I could pour my heart into. Web Design had really caught my interest, and I had started conversations with designers to see how I should go about finding my place. I was told to check out a school called General Assembly, and it was there that I first heard about UX Design.


What drew me to UX Design was that it was creative, but also tailored. I saw potential in it that was really unlimited for creating great designs that could make a serious impact. I decided to pursue UX/UI Design through BLOC’s Immersive Apprenticeship, and the knowledge, experience, and rapid growth that I realized further confirmed that I was on the right path. Being immersed in surveys, mind mapping, sketches, wire framing, coding, etc. gave me a huge appreciation for quality design, and a determination to make my mark as a Designer.


Currently I am looking to join a team that is as passionate about quality design as I am, where I can collaborate with other designers and create intuitive, impactful designs. Whether you are looking for someone to join your team, or have a project I can help with, I’d love to hear from you. Thanks for taking a look at my work!
